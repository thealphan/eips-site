About

EIPS is a specialist systems engineering and safety consultancy led by Chartered Engineer Charlie Jenkins. With deep technical grounding and cross-sector expertise, we support the development and certification of complex, safety-critical systems across aerospace, defence, maritime, and transport domains.

Our approach combines hands-on engineering rigour with strategic oversight, helping clients meet both regulatory expectations and operational goals with confidence.

Services

  • Safety lifecycle delivery: FHA, FTA, PRA, CMA, SSA
  • Reliability modelling and analysis
  • Hazard identification and risk classification frameworks
  • Assurance strategy and certification planning
  • Support for SRR, PDR, CDR and safety boards

Portfolio

  • Safety Signatory for ATA32 systems on Airbus platforms (A350, A380, A330)
  • Safety and assurance lead on novel landing gear and UAS programmes
  • Fault tree modelling for high-integrity naval and aerospace systems
  • Cross-national coordination of multi-platform safety deliverables

Technical Capabilities

  • Systems safety methodologies: ARP4754A, ARP4761, CS-25, DEF-STAN 00-56
  • Model-Based Safety Analysis (MBSA)
  • Tools: Python, MATLAB, Reliability Workbench, CATIA V5
  • Agile and V-model lifecycle integration
