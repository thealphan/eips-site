About EIPS

EIPS (Engineering In Practice Solutions) is a systems safety and design consultancy, led by Chartered Engineer Charlie Jenkins. We specialise in bridging the gap between engineering intent and operational assurance, with services across aerospace, defence, maritime, and high-integrity sectors.

We combine practical insight with regulatory depth — supporting safety cases, system design, functional modelling, and certification artefacts with hands-on expertise.
